In the novel, A Christmas Carol by Charles Dickinson, the ghost of the banker Jacob Marley returns to visit his former business parther Ebenezer Scrooge. Jacob comes to deliver a warning message to Scrooge that may enable Scrooge a chance and a hope to repent and avoid the terrible fate that is being experienced by Jacob.

May we learn these powerful truths and better apply them in our lives.
